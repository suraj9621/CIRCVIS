How It Works

From Image to Insight in 3 Steps

1

Upload or Capture

Drop any waste image or use your live camera. CIRCVIS supports JPEG, PNG, WebP up to 10MB and real-time video streams from any device camera.

2

Ensemble AI Analysis

Three deep learning models (ResNet50, MobileNetV2, EfficientNetB0) simultaneously analyse your image. A soft-voting ensemble combines their predictions for 89% accuracy.

3

Actionable Results

Get per-class confidence scores, CO₂ impact estimates, material economic value, and a full recycling recommendation — all in under 200ms.
